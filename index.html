Ph.D. Student in Statistics @ UC Santa Barbara

Hi! I am a Ph.D. student in the Department of Statistics and Applied Probability at the University of California, Santa Barbara. My current research interests include Gaussian processes, kernel methods, scientific machine learning, and numerical methods for differential equations and density functional theory.

Recently, I have been working on Gaussian-process-based solvers for nonlinear PDEs, additive Schwarz preconditioning for kernel matrices, and neural/operator-learning approaches for one-dimensional hard-rod classical density functional theory.
